# Dt Method Poly Items

TOPOLOGY:
    

Topology.

Copy from identical topology meshes.

NEAREST:
    

Nearest Face.

Copy from nearest face (using center points).

NORMAL:
    

Best Normal-Matching.

Copy from source face which normal is the closest to destination one.

POLYINTERP_PNORPROJ:
    

Projected Face Interpolated.

Interpolate all source polygons intersected by the projection of destination one along its own normal.
